---
name: "Parallax"
description: "Tool for viewing any news story from three different cultural perspectives."
status: "In Progress"
tech: "React, Claude API"
---

Parallax takes a news headline or URL and generates three different perspectives on the same story — not political left/right/center, but cultural perspectives from different parts of the world. How might this story read in a newspaper in Lagos, in a tech blog in Seoul, or in a community newsletter in rural France?

The goal isn't to tell you which perspective is "right." It's to make the invisible frame around every story suddenly visible. We all read the news through a lens shaped by geography, language, and culture. Parallax makes that lens tangible.

Built with React on the frontend and Claude's API for perspective generation, with careful prompt engineering to avoid caricature and prioritize nuance.
