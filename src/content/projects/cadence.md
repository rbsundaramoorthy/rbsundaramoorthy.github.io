Cadence is an idea for a developer productivity tool that replaces the sprint model with something more organic. Instead of two-week boxes, Cadence tracks your natural work rhythms — when you tend to do deep work, when you context-switch, when you're most creative versus most analytical — and suggests task scheduling based on those patterns.

The hypothesis is that most developers already have a natural cadence to their work, but project management tools fight against it instead of working with it. What if the tool adapted to you instead of the other way around?

Still in the idea stage. Exploring Rust for the implementation because the tool needs to be fast enough to feel invisible.
